My top 3 craft hobbies of all time

Fiber art

Fiber art is one of my favorites because it encompasses many different crafts such as: crochet, knitting, cross stitching, and embroidery.

Digital Art

Digital art interests me greatly because of how easy it is to alter and try new things without having to completely start a project over.

Painting

Painting is on its own because there are many different kinds of painting such as watercolor, acrylic, oil, etc. I don't have a distinct preference because I liek the different painting mediums for different projects.
